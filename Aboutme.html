About Me

Some Interesting Stuff About Me

For many years, I have been building and repairing computers. The first computer I ever built was based on the Intel LGA 775 processor. Everything from motherboards, processors, memory sticks, optical drives, various coolers and thermal pastes, power supplies to hard drives including: IDE, SATA and SSD, I have tested and assembled computers with all of them. My experience in programming was of course limited while doing all of this.


One day while surfing the internet I came across an advertisment for Python computer programming. After trying out a few exercises, I was hooked and wanted to go further. That in turn, made me curious about websites and how they function. After getting into the CUNY TechWorks Program, I started to learn about HTML5, CSS, Javascript and JQuery. All of these are crucial components in a website's functionality.


While I don't consider myself an expert, I do grasp the material enough to produce functional work. They say: "practice makes perfect". As time goes on, I aim to come close to the level of a professional web developer. Will have to keep at it in order to attain that goal.
