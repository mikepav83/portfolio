ET-710 Web Technology:

Building and Maintaining Web Sites

In this gallery, you will find samples of my work done for the ET-710 class.

"HTML Tables and Forms"
The tables and form here are made with pure html. Got the inspiration from the CUNY Portal website and added my own twist using several different shades of blue. I feel the contrast makes the finished product look very attractive. Especially since it is just some tables and a form.
"Magnificent Views of New York Skylines"
Used Dreamweaver to make this piece. Having lived in NYC my whole life, the skyline here is incredibly beautiful no matter what time of the day it is. The only downside was not being able to find higher resolution images of the exact same skyline view during the night and day cycle.
"Video Advertisement"
Made a video advertisement with this gorgeous Mercedes Benz sports car. It seems a little cheesy but in the end, I did like the final product and did not make any changes. Wonder if the folks at Mercedes Benz like it? Perhaps I should send them a link. Will have to see about that.
"Tribute to the Birth of America"
This was a piece inspired by The Declaration of Independence. Even though this country is 242 years old, which is young in comparison to other nations, it is still one of the best countries to live and work in. Many people consider it the land of opportunity. Hard to argue with that.
"Image Map"
Since I do enjoy golf, I had to make an image map of one of the most famous golf courses in the world. St. Andrews Links has one of the first and oldest golf courses in the entire world. It is also a very scenic place to boot. If you enjoy golf, then this place should be on your list of places to travel to.
"First Website"
This is one of the very first attempts I made in creating a website of my own. There isn't much in terms of content, but I was just getting into HTML and CSS back then. It is good to see how far I have come as time went on during the course of the semester.
